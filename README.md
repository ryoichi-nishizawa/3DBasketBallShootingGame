# 3DBasketBallShootingGame

A 3D basketball shooting game built with Unity.
Featuring smooth ball physics and shooting assistance for a better game feel.

## Key Features
* **Shoot Assistance**: Smart trajectory correction near the hoop.
* **Physics-based Gameplay**: Realistic ball movement and bounce.
* **Refined Controls**: Precise positioning for shooting.

## How to Play
1. Launch the executable (.exe).
2. Move to the desired position.
3. Shoot and aim for the high score!

### **Controls**
* **Left Mouse Button (LMB)**:
    * **No ball**: Spawn a ball at your feet.
    * **Ball at feet**: Pick up the ball to your hand.
    * **Ball in hand**: Hold the button to charge your shot power, then release to shoot.
* **Arrow Keys**: Move the player (available only when not holding a ball).
* **Right Mouse Button (RMB) + Move Mouse**: Rotate the camera. 
    * *Note: The ball will fly in the direction the camera is facing.*
* **Alt + F4**: Exit the game.

### **Pro Tips / Strategy**
Want a quick score? 
Don't move from the starting position, charge your power to the **MAX**, and release. There's a high probability it'll go straight into the hoop! Give it a try—it's incredibly satisfying!
